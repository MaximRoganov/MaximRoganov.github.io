Not sure what kind of photo you need?

Not a problem!

Every factor is taken into account to make sure your photos reflect your
character and send the right message.

The styles we have to choose from are as follows

Studio-style

The results are similar to what you would get in a studio setting. You are shot against a plain background, so focus is drawn directly to you.

Office

You are photographed with an office background. This is a slightly more realistic rendition, where your image is associated with the working environment which you are a part of.

Location

We head outside, or to a window. London is richly populated with iconic architecture that can be used to express your local identity.

Informal

Perhaps you do not want a traditional portrait-style image. To achieve a more spontaneous look, we will invite a colleague to chat with you, or ask you to engage in an activity.

What else?

Your time used well.

We allocate up to an hour for each individual shoot, although many sessions don’t take any longer than 30 minutes.

This is done to ensure that your time is well spent. Everybody has a different attitude to getting their photo taken. Some require more time to relax and find a natural pose that works best for them. This is also done to make sure that if you want to try different clothing options, such as a different shirt, different tie, etc. - There is ample time to change and take the shots. If we finish early - you have a few extra minutes to catch up on work or to take a break.

Convenient Logistics.

The shoot will take place at or near your office. There is no need to plan travel, or spend valuable time on a train or in a car. We come to you.

Efficient Technique.

Many corporate photographers will use large, cumbersome equipment that may be better suited for glamour photography and magazine covers, but in fact offers little advantage over more modest and mobile setups. We are not interested in impressing you with our kit, only with our results. Our equipment is tailored to the service we provide, saving space and electricity, without compromising on quality.

Professional Retouching

Our photographs are beautiful and striking right out of the camera, however every great image that you see has been carefully optimised for a discerning viewership. You only get to make a first impression once, so we make absolutely certain that the photograph you put out there is as perfect as it can be.
